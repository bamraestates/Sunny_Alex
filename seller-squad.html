Kelly: "How do I know what price to list my home at? I don’t want to underprice or overprice."

Kelly: "How can I get my home noticed? What about photos and marketing?"

Step 5 – Reviewing Offers

Kelly: "What happens once offers come in? How do I know which one to choose?"

Me: "We’ll review each offer carefully, including price, terms, and the buyer’s ability to close. It’s all about the right fit."

Step 6 – Closing the Deal

Kelly: "It’s almost time to close! What happens now?"

Me: "We’ll finalize everything. Once we sign, the keys are officially handed over to the new owners."
